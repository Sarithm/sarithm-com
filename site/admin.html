OnDemand Administrators

To simplify the process we break down System Administrators into two categories, OnGoing and OnDemand. We will cut down your IT infrastructure maintenance cost by half using OnDemand & OnGoing approach. Our OnDemand Administrators will handle tasks that include Software and Hardware updates, patch management, and migrations. Your System Administrators work on day to day tasks. For our role, we will evaluate your needs and provide estimates for the time needed to day tasks. For our role, we will evaluate your needs and provide estimates for the time needed to complete each task.

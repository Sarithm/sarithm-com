MINICASH

Have you ever needed to spilt shared expenses or lent an item to someone then forgot? It is a common occurrence that happens to all of us. Family events and weekend trips can add up and it is often difficult to remember what we may owe others money or that others owe us. We also often lose track of items we have lent out after a time has passed. Beyond all that it is difficult to ask for money or a borrowed item to be returned.

We have developed an app just for those reasons. MiniCash not only keeps track of shared expenses across a multitude of reasons, but also allows you to send a friendly reminder to others that they need to return a borrowed item. Our app has taken out the awkwardness and given you the power to keep track of your money and personal belongings.

After all, we have other things to worry about.

We believe that the infrastructure transparency leads to higher efficiency in infrastructure management. A solid understanding of our infrastructure in the beginning stages is a manageable task, though we often tend to lose this understanding as it begins to grow. This creates the need for a resource that allows us to keep track of all dynamic infrastructure changes. Our best practices will allow you to do just that. Your System Administrator can now keep track of all changes, addressing each one as it happens, which results in a secured, cost effective, and well managed infrastructure.
